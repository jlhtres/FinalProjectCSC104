Food App Design – Wireframe

In the wireframe stage of my UX design process for the food app, I focused on laying out the core structure and navigation without visual distractions. Screens cover browsing meals, clipping coupons, and accessing the pharmacy, ensuring the user journey is clear and tasks are easy to complete.

High-Fidelity Design

After finalizing the wireframes, I moved into visual design—bringing the app to life with color, typography, and imagery. A clean, appetizing palette and refined button placement guide users smoothly through shopping and pharmacy requests.
